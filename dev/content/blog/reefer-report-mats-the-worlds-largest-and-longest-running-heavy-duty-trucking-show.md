The Mid-America Trucking Show (MATS) is the biggest and longest-running event in the heavy-duty trucking world. Each year, Kentucky becomes the heart of the trucking industry by hosting over 56,000 attendees. This event brings together leaders in the trucking sector, from manufacturers to owner-operators, offering unparalleled access to new technologies, products, and educational opportunities. The event will take place from March 27 to March 29, 2025, at the Kentucky Exposition Center in Louisville, Kentucky.

### History of MATS: A Tradition of Innovation

The Mid-America Trucking Show has a rich history that dates back to its founding in 1972. The event was founded by Paul K. Young, former president of the Kentucky Motor Transport Association, alongside a group of industry enthusiasts and professionals. Initially, the show was designed to bring together industry players for networking and learning, and it has since grown into the premier event for the heavy-duty trucking industry.

For more than 50 years, MATS has remained a crucial meeting point for trucking professionals, offering a dynamic space where exhibitors and attendees can connect. Over the decades, MATS has evolved alongside the trucking industry. It has incorporated new technology, regulatory changes, and shifting market demands. Today, the show draws thousands from across the globe. This includes manufacturers, service providers, heavy duty truck drivers, and fleet owners. As a result, MATS has become a must-attend event.

### Educational Opportunities at MATS 2025

MATS isn't just a showcase of trucks and technology but it's also a hub for education. The Pro Talks Education Series is a significant feature of the event, offering dozens of seminars that cover a range of topics affecting the trucking industry. These seminars provide in-depth insights into current issues, new trends, and practical solutions for every segment of the industry.

#### Pro Talks Education Series

The Pro Talks Education Series is an educational platform that addresses important topics such as:

- **Emerging Technologies**: As electric trucks and alternative fuels gain importance, MATS 2025 will spotlight the future of energy-efficient trucks. Attendees can explore the latest innovations in sustainable trucking solutions and heavy duty truck ramps.
- **Regulatory Changes**: The trucking industry is highly regulated, and changes in laws and policies can have significant impacts on operators. The Pro Talks series will feature discussions on new regulations, including compliance with the Electronic Logging Devices (ELD) mandate and hours of service (HOS) rules.
- **Owner-Operator Strategies**: Small fleet owners and owner-operators are a significant part of the trucking world. MATS will offer seminars designed to help these professionals manage costs, improve efficiency, and stay ahead of industry trends.

### Special Events and Attractions at MATS 2025

MATS is not only about business but it's also about showcasing the culture and excitement of the trucking world. Throughout the event, there will be several special events that are sure to entertain and inspire attendees.

#### PKY Truck Beauty Championship

One of the most highly anticipated events at MATS is the PKY Truck Beauty Championship, where custom trucks compete for the title of the most beautifully designed rig. Located outside the Expo Center in Lot N, this competition features a dazzling array of customized trucks that highlight the creativity and craftsmanship of the trucking community.

#### Big Rig Build-Off

Another crowd favorite is the Big Rig Build-Off, which features five custom truck builders who go head-to-head in a race to create the most impressive truck. This competition not only showcases the skill of the builders but also gives attendees a glimpse into the creativity and craftsmanship that goes into making a custom rig.

#### Truck Parking Community

MATS 2025 will also offer a Truck Parking Community for truckers attending the show. This dedicated parking area will feature amenities like free coffee and a hospitality tent to make the experience more comfortable for drivers who are there to participate in the event.

### Exhibitor Events and Interactive Experiences

MATS 2025 will be full of interactive activities that offer fun, networking, and even the chance to give back to the community.
