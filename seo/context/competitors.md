## Authority reality (Semrush)
techrig Authority Score = 10 (175 referring domains): the weakest in its competitive set. Closest beatable peer authorityexpressllc (AS 16). Dispatch peers logity (AS 23) and jjkeller (AS 42) are stronger. Implication: win on long-tail + content quality, not on domain strength. Do not target head terms expecting to outrank gov/marketplaces.

## Where to compete
- **Compliance/setup:** the commercial SERP is wide open (gov + forums + a few mid-tier services). Win with structured, specific, owner-operator-friendly content: cost/fee tables, exact timelines ("how long does X take"), step-by-step authority guides, strong FAQ. Out-specific the .gov pages; out-help the dry enterprise pages.
- **Dispatch:** deepen the box truck franchise (relative strength) before spreading across trailer types.

## The one angle no competitor can copy (use it everywhere)
The **integrated setup → dispatch funnel**: techrig files your authority/compliance AND dispatches you afterward, long term. Compliance-only players (Authority Express, J.J. Keller) and dispatch-only players (Logity, Max Dispatch) each do half. Pair this with **transparent fixed pricing** (BOC-3 $100, etc.) and **no contracts / no forced dispatch**. Lead money pages with this thesis.
