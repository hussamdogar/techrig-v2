# SEO & Content Strategy: DGR Tech Rig

Framed around upgrading existing assets, not building from zero. Synthesizes Phases 1-7. Locale US/USD.

## Positioning angle (the thesis every page sells)
**"We set up your trucking company, then keep it loaded for years."** The one position no competitor holds: compliance-only players (Authority Express, J.J. Keller) and dispatch-only players (Logity, Max Dispatch) each do half. Tech Rig runs the whole journey — file your authority, then dispatch your truck. Reinforced by two proofs competitors avoid: **transparent fixed pricing** (BOC-3 $100, UCR tiers published — competitors hide pricing) and **no contracts / no forced dispatch**.

Strategic posture given Authority Score 10: win on **low-competition, high-intent compliance terms + genuinely better content**, not on domain strength. Treat link-building as a parallel long-game, never a launch dependency.

## Content pillars
1. **Get on the road (compliance & setup)** — acquisition. Authority filing, registrations, company formation. Greenfield; highest priority.
2. **Stay loaded (dispatch)** — retention. Trailer-type dispatch, led by the box truck franchise.
3. **The bridge** — guides that move a reader from "starting out" to "being dispatched" (e.g. box truck business → box truck dispatch).
4. **Trucking know-how (authority/blog)** — TOFU education that feeds pillars 1-2 via internal links and builds topical authority.

## Repurposing plan
- **Landing pages:** the compliance money pages double as the destinations for any future paid campaigns (the old Ads pages' role, now organic-first).
- **LinkedIn / founder:** turn the "real order of operations" and "first-year compliance checklist" into founder posts; operational client stories (pending `experience-notes.md`) as proof posts.
- **Instagram / short video:** the SERP shows YouTube ranking for "how to get your USDOT & MC" — repurpose pillar steps into short explainers (hands to Design/Dev; SEO specifies topics).
- **Email/newsletter:** compliance-deadline reminders (UCR season, biennial update) to the existing client base — retention + upsell into dispatch.
- **Sales enablement:** the bundle page + transparent pricing becomes the sales one-pager.

## Measurement framework
- **Baseline (Phase 3, 12 mo):** ~600 clicks, ~281k impressions, CTR 0.22%, avg pos 27; GA4 5,238 sessions, 0 tracked conversions.
- **Fix first (Dev handoff):** GA4 key events — generate_lead, BOC-3 start/complete, click-to-call, WhatsApp, consultation; cross-domain to `form.`/`boc-3.` (see migration-plan §8). Without this, ROI is unmeasurable.
- **KPIs:** (1) compliance money pages entering the index and earning first impressions (pivot working); (2) non-brand organic clicks up, CTR off the 0.22% floor (consolidation working); (3) box truck franchise holds/improves (defense); (4) key-event conversions by landing page and channel (the real goal once tracking exists).
- **Cadence:** weekly GSC vs baseline for the PRESERVE pages (regression watch) for 60 days post-launch; monthly thereafter via the Operations Track (System 3 audit, System 4 refresh).
- **Leading indicator:** the existing 14k-impression DOT/MC demand and the high-engagement compliance landing traffic should convert to organic clicks once those pages are indexable and content-complete.
