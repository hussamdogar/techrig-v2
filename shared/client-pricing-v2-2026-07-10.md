# Tech Rig Website — Final Pricing, Bundle, and Content Changes

## 1. Core pricing rule

Use two prices for services:

1. Standalone price — when a customer buys one service by itself.
2. Bundle price — when the service is included inside one of the compliance bundles.

The final bundle price is the sum of the approved discounted in-bundle service prices, rounded to the nearest $100 for a clean customer-facing price point. The itemized total (exact sum before rounding) is shown in each package's included-services table for transparency; the rounding adjustment is called out as a separate line.

- Do not add a convenience fee.
- Do not apply an extra package-level discount beyond the listed in-bundle service discounts and the rounding adjustment noted per bundle.

## 21. Refund policy

Remove the old flat $50 processing fee.

For eligible refunds, deduct only the actual non-refundable payment-processing fee.

Example: If the customer paid $100 and the non-refundable payment-processing fee was $3, the refund is $97.

**Full refund eligibility:**
- No work started
- No filing submitted
- No consultation or service delivered
- Request made within 3 business days

**Partial refund:** Up to 75% may be considered if preparation started but no filing was submitted.

**Non-refundable cases:**
- Filing already submitted
- Substantial work completed
- Third-party or government fees committed
- Client inactive for 30 days due to missing documents or delayed response

Policy last updated: June 25, 2026.
