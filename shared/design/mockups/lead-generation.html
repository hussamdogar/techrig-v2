The filter

Why brokers avoid new authority

Many brokers screen on authority age to limit risk, so a new MC (the "30-day authority" stage) gets fewer yeses.

It is not personal, it is a filter.

The way past a filter is a combination of the right broker relationships and a clean, growing track record.
