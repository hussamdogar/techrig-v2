11 · FAQ

Scannable, Grade 8

Is UCR a one-time fee?
No. UCR is annual. You renew every year, usually before the new registration year opens. BOC-3, by contrast, is generally one-time.
What happens if I do not pay UCR?
In states that enforce it, you can be fined at roadside and your vehicle held until it's resolved. Renewing on time avoids this.
Do I pay the government fee to Tech Rig?
The government fee is separate and goes to the program. Our service fee is for filing it correctly and on time. You see both before you pay.

14 · Guardrails

What we do, and don't

Do

Navy + one rationed amber. Real proof only. Content-bearing cards. SVG-first for speed. The status tracker as the anchor. AA contrast everywhere. Reduced motion respected.

Don't

Red-on-black, stock highway photos, undocumented metrics, white text on amber, star/rating widgets, FMCSA "seals", gradient text, glassmorphism, fade-up-on-everything, a third founder.
