### H2: What our box truck dispatch service covers
- Load matching for box truck lanes, including the regional and local work box trucks run.
- Rate negotiation so you are not taking the first number a broker offers.
- Route planning to keep utilization high and empty miles down.
- Billing and paperwork, rate confirmations, and the documents your factoring needs.
- Support when a load or a lane changes.

### H2: Built for box truck owner-operators
Box-truck-specific framing (the page's unique angle): box truck operators often juggle more, shorter loads than over-the-road carriers, and chasing those loads yourself eats the day. We watch the boards and the broker relationships so you can drive. Whether you run one box truck or a few, the dispatch is tuned to how box trucks actually earn. New to box trucks? See our [guide to starting a box truck business](/how-to-start-a-box-truck-business/).

### H2: Box truck dispatch pricing
We charge 8% of gross monthly revenue for box truck dispatch. No long-term contract, no forced dispatch, no lock-in. See typical numbers on our [box truck dispatch cost](/box-truck-dispatch/cost/) page. We earn a percentage, so keeping your rates and utilization high is our job too.

Funnel cross-link. Not active yet? We can get your box truck legally set up: USDOT, MC authority, BOC-3, insurance, and the rest. See [compliance services](/compliance-services/). The same team that gets you road-legal keeps you loaded after.
