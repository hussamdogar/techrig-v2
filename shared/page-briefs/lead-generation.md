## Heading outline + copy

### H1: Finding Brokers That Work With New Authority
**Hero lede:**
Getting your authority is the first hurdle. The second one surprises new carriers: brokers that want to see 6 months or a year of authority before they will give you a load. With a brand-new MC, you can feel locked out of the freight you just got legal to haul. We help new-authority carriers get past that, by finding the brokers and loads that work with new MCs and by dispatching you so you build a track record fast.

**Primary CTA (above fold):** button "Get loads on new authority" → `/services/` or `/contact-us/`. [VERIFY route.]

### H2: Why brokers avoid new authority
Plain explanation: many brokers screen on authority age to limit risk, so a new MC (the "30-day authority" stage) gets fewer yeses. It is not personal, it is a filter. The way past a filter is a combination of the right broker relationships and a clean, growing track record.

### H2: How we help new-authority carriers get loads
- We point you to brokers and load sources that work with new authority.
- We dispatch you so loads start moving and your authority builds history.
- We make sure your compliance is airtight, because a clean record is part of what gets you approved (see [compliance services](/compliance-services/)).

### H2: New authority? Start at the source
Funnel both directions. If you do not have your authority yet, we set it up: see [compliance services](/compliance-services/) or the [guide to starting a trucking company](/how-to-start-a-trucking-company/). Already active? Our [dispatch service](/services/) keeps you loaded while your authority ages into more broker approvals.

### H2: New authority loads FAQ
FAQPage schema, Grade 8:
- "Why won't brokers give me loads on new authority?" Many screen on authority age to limit risk. New MCs get fewer approvals at first.
- "How long until more brokers work with me?" It varies, and we cannot promise a timeline, but building a clean track record steadily opens more doors.
- "Can you both set up my authority and find me loads?" Yes. That is the advantage of one team: [compliance](/compliance-services/) plus [dispatch](/services/).
